FAQ

Common Questions

How much does coaching cost? +
Programs start from $129/month for nutrition-only coaching, up to $349+/month for full competition prep. Pricing is discussed during the discovery call based on your specific needs and program. There are no hidden fees.
Is coaching available remotely or in-person only? +
All coaching is delivered online. You get the same personalised attention and results regardless of your location. I coach athletes across multiple countries and time zones.
How quickly will I see results? +
Most clients notice meaningful physical and performance changes within 4–6 weeks. Significant body composition changes typically emerge at 8–12 weeks with full commitment. Results depend on consistency, adherence to the program, and your individual physiology.
What if I don't have gym access or equipment? +
Programs can be adapted for home training, limited equipment, or specific gym settings. Just let me know your situation in the application and I'll design accordingly.
Do you offer nutrition coaching without a training plan? +
Yes — the Macro Mastery program is nutrition-only. It's a great option if you already have a solid training routine but want to dial in your diet for better performance or body composition.
